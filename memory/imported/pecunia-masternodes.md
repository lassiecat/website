**Formula:**
```

```

- Collateral: 10,000 coins at $1 = $10,000

## RED FLAGS FOR CHEAP MASTERNODES

**Be extremely cautious of:**

1. **New coins under $1 with masternodes**
   - Often pump-and-dump schemes
   - Low liquidity = can't sell

2. **Promises of 50%+ monthly ROI**
   - Unsustainable
   - Likely scam or ponzi

3. **No active development**
   - Check GitHub activity
   - No recent commits = dead project

4. **Anonymous developers**
   - Legitimate projects have known teams
   - Privacy coins are exception (DASH, PIVX)

5. **Forced buy-in**
   - Must buy coins from specific exchange
   - Limited trading pairs
